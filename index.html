OVERVIEW

As per the Nigeria Bureau of Statistics (NBS), unemployment affects 40.1% of Nigerians. Given the nation
population of over 200 million, this translates to approximately 80.2 million individuals without employment.
In response to this, Tech Diversity by Instincthub has been designed to annually train 100,000 individuals,
with the aim of securing direct employment for 20% of them. This initiative is expected to generate wealth
for families who have been excluded from employment opportunities
